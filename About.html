ABOUT UTRAX

Utrax auto motors was founded three years ago by manuh Spice with an aim of motorising Africa. He started it as a small organisation but later it gained popularity and joined up with other organisations to form the now popular utrax.


Utrax is now africa's leading automobile company with branches all over the African continent.It is also famous becouse it sells all types of cars together with their spare parts.It also offers free three months full license cover for all automobiles sold. All cars bought from utrax have already installed GPS tracker that can be linked with your phone to track every movement of your car. The cars are automated with the latest technology and powerful pioneer music systeem,in addition it has both customized interior with eighter leather or rexin.
